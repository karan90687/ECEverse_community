Job Description

We are looking for a skilled VLSI Design Engineer to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for designing and implementing complex digital integrated circuits using state-of-the-art tools and methodologies.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design and develop digital circuits using Verilog/SystemVerilog
  • Perform RTL coding, simulation, and synthesis
  • Collaborate with verification team to ensure design quality
  • Participate in physical design and timing closure activities
  • Work on power optimization and area reduction techniques
  • Create and maintain design documentation

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Electronics/Electrical Engineering
  • Strong understanding of digital design concepts and CMOS technology
  • Experience with industry-standard EDA tools (Cadence, Synopsys, Mentor)
  • Knowledge of scripting languages like Perl, Python, or Tcl
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills
